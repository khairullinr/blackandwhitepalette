BNWP Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16.04.2026

1. Who We Are

This app is provided by an independent developer.
Contact: rikh.development@gmail.com

2. Photos, Editing, and Face-Related Processing

BNWP is an offline photo editor. Photos you select or capture stay on your device. We do not upload your photos or edits to our servers. Edits, effects, and AI features (if used) run on-device.

Some editing features, such as local face retouch, may analyze a downscaled copy of a photo selected by you in order to estimate an approximate face area from image pixels so the chosen effect can be applied locally. This processing happens entirely on-device.

We do not create or store a face mesh, facial landmarks, facial recognition template, biometric identifier, or any face profile used to identify a person. We do not use face-related processing for identification, authentication, advertising, analytics, profiling, or tracking.

3. Data We Process

We do not require an account and we do not collect your name, phone number, or precise location.

The app may store locally on your device:

  • App settings and preferences.
  • Editing project metadata (for text/shapes/effects) and thumbnail/preview caches.
  • Your imported photos, edited exports, and local project files that you choose to keep on your device.

If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and message.

In-app purchases:

  • PRO is offered as a paid in-app purchase.
  • Purchases are processed by Apple/Google. We do not receive your payment details.
  • The store may provide us with purchase status and transaction identifiers needed to unlock PRO. We store PRO status locally on your device.

4. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data or face data with third parties, except if required by law or to respond to your support request.

If you choose to share an exported photo using your device’s share features, the recipient service will process the file under its own policies.

5. Retention and Deletion

We do not retain face data on our servers.

Any temporary face-related processing data used to apply an effect exists only on the device during editing and is discarded when no longer needed.

User photos, edited outputs, caches, and local project data remain on your device until you delete them, clear app data, use the in-app cache cleanup tools, or uninstall the app.

6. Permissions

Camera: to capture photos.

Photo library/media: to pick photos, compare existing media for cache cleanup, and save exported results, depending on the permissions you grant.

7. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will show the updated text in the app with a new “Last updated” date.
